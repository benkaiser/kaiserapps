About Me

Software engineer, open-source builder, and dad based on the Gold Coast, Australia.

I'm a Principal Software Engineer at Microsoft on the SharePoint team. Before that, I completed my Master's degree in Software Engineering by age 20, and in 2013 I was one of the Grand Prize Winners of Google Code-in, contributing to KDE's Marble project.

Outside of my day job, I'm a prolific open-source builder with 180+ public repositories on GitHub. I love exploring new technology and building tools that solve real problems.

I'm passionate about open source, making AI accessible to everyone, building safe technology for my kids, and my Christian faith. I also offer mentoring through MentorCruise.
